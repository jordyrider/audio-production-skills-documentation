```
Reference schema for all genre skill files.

## Sonic DNA

## Prompting Patterns
Effective phrase combinations and descriptors that consistently produce this genre in AI music generation. Include genre labels, BPM hints, mood words, and arrangement terms. Add examples in the format: `phrase/term → what it emphasises`.

Full prompts tested and confirmed to work well. Format: `prompt → description of actual output`. Leave empty until validated through real usage testing.

## Known Limitations
What this genre consistently struggles with or fails to produce accurately. Leave empty until discovered through usage.

## Still Testing
```

```
# Voice Category Template

## Purpose
Reference schema for all voice category skill files.

## Status: stub

## Purpose
What vocal style this category covers and when to use it. Name the content type, platform, tone goal, and emotional register.

## Delivery Style
Pace, tone, energy level, register, character notes. Describe what makes this category distinct from adjacent ones (e.g. how hook-openers differ from cta-promo). 3–5 bullet points.

## Example Lines
Representative script lines demonstrating the range within this category. Aim for 5+ lines; hook-openers has 13 specific lines.

## ElevenLabs Voice Recommendations
Specific voice names, IDs, or voice characteristics (age, gender, accent, tone) that work well for this category.

## ElevenLabs Settings
Stability, similarity boost, style exaggeration, and speaker boost settings. Note defaults vs. adjusted values and when to deviate.

## ElevenLabs Notes
Model-specific tips, caveats, or consistent behaviours for this voice category. Update from real usage.
```
