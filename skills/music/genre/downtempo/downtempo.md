# Downtempo / Ambient

Super-genre descriptor for the Downtempo / Ambient cluster. Read this first to orient within the cluster, then load the specific sub-genre file.

## Status: stub

---

## Scope
Downtempo / Ambient covers music defined by slow tempos, atmospheric texture, and intentional restraint. It ranges from beat-driven styles like lo-fi hip-hop and trip-hop (where rhythm is present but unhurried) to fully beatless ambient and drone music. The cluster is defined by mood over momentum — music meant to be felt, not danced to.
