## Scope
Alternative / Indie Rock covers guitar-driven music that emerged as a reaction to mainstream rock and pop — characterised by independence from major label aesthetics, DIY ethos, and a willingness to blend genres. It spans from jangly 80s college rock and 90s grunge through indie pop, dream pop, shoegaze, and the synth-inflected sounds of the 2000s onward. Broad enough to include lo-fi bedroom recordings and polished indie-pop.
