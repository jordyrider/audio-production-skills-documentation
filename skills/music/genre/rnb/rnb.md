## Scope
R&B is a broad umbrella covering African-American popular music rooted in blues, gospel, and jazz. It spans from the raw electric blues of the 1940s through soul and funk, into the polished new jack swing of the 80s–90s, and contemporary urban R&B of the 2000s onward. The cluster is defined by groove, feel, and vocal expression over harmonic complexity.
